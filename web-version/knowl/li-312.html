Item 2.

Define the two functions \(g:\mathbb{Z}_2{}^3\rightarrow \mathbb{Z}_2{}^4\) and \(p:\mathbb{Z}_2{}^4\to \mathbb{Z}_2\) by \(g(a_1,a_2,a_3) = (a_1,a_2,a_3 ,a_1+_2 a_2+_2a_3)\text{,}\) and \(p(b_1,b_2,b_3,b_4)=b_1+b_2+b_3+b_4\text{.}\)

  1. The values of \(g(a_1,a_2,a_3)\) and \(p(b_1,b_2,b_3,b_4)\) can be computed as matrix products. Determine the matrices.

  2. Describe the function \(p \circ g\text{.}\) Is it a homomorphism?
