The Hamming Distance, \(d_H\text{,}\) between two strings of bits with the same length is the number positions within the two strings where the strings differ. For instance, \(d_H(11101,10100)=2\) because the two strings differ in positions 2 and 5. What is the minimal Hamming distance between any two strings in the set

\begin{equation*} C =\{00000,11011,11100,00111\}? \end{equation*}
