Item 4.
Linear Codes. Let \(m\) and \(n\) be positive integers. Suppose that \(G\) is an \(m \times n\) matrix of zeros and ones. Then we can can define a function \(e:\mathbb{Z}_2^m \rightarrow \mathbb{Z}_2^n\) by \(e(a)=a G\) where we take \(a \in \mathbb{Z}_2^m\) to be a \(1\times m\) matrix and we perform mod 2 arithmetic in computing the product \(a G\text{.}\) Prove that \(e\) is a homomorphism.
