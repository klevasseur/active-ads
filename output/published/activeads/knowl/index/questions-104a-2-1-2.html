Exercise 2.
The graph \(G\) has vertices with degree sequence \((5, 3, 3, 2, 2, 1)\text{.}\) How many edges does it have? If it is planar, into how many regions of the plane does it divide? Does \(G\) have an Eulerian path?
Answer.
The degree sequence indicates that \(v=6\) and \(e=8\text{.}\) Since \(v +r - e=6+r-8=2\text{,}\) \(r = 4\text{.}\)
