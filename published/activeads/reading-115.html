    Section 40.1 Reading Assignment

    Read Section 13.3 of Applied Discrete Structures. In this section we further restrict our attention to lattices that have the algebraic properties that are required of a Boolean Algebra. Propositional logic that we saw in Chapter 3 is prime model for what Boolean Algebra is like, but there several other concrete Boolean Algebras that we will consider.
